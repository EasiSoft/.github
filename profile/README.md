## Hi there 👋

At **EasiSoft**, we believe software is better when it's free.  
Our mission is to build and share software that anyone can use, learn from, and improve.

We value:
- **Open source collaboration** — ideas grow stronger when shared.  
- **Simplicity and clarity** — clean code, minimal boilerplate, and practical design.  
- **Accessibility** — making technology approachable for learners, hobbyists, and professionals alike.  

### What we do
We work mainly in **C and C++**, creating:
- **System-level tools** — libraries, modules, and utilities.  
- **General software projects** — anything that sparks curiosity from our developers and community.
